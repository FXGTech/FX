About Us

Since 2008, Fuxiang Technology has leveraged advanced 3D CAD technology to manufacture high-quality components for PVD, CVD, and vacuum automation equipment. We work with semiconductor labs, research institutes such as ASTAR and SUTD, and provide cost-effective solutions across high-tech applications.

Our team specializes in high-temperature Viton-based designs, ensuring reliability in automation and research environments.
