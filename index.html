A Singapore engineering firm

Established Since 2008

Singapore-based company that specializes in the re-engineering and manufacturing of hardware parts for the vacuum-sealed chamber process.

We offer expertise in PVD sputtering deposition and use the latest 3D CAD technology to design and fabricate custom equipment parts for various industries. As an engineering firm, we are dedicated to providing cost-competitive manufacturing and strong engineering capabilities to our customers. Our goal is to be a valued partner in the industry by delivering innovative and high-quality hardware solutions.

Our Products

Tungsten Filament

We offer high-quality tungsten filaments for various applications in vacuum chambers. Our filaments are durable and designed to withstand high temperatures.

Shield

Our shields provide protection and containment for delicate components within vacuum chambers. They are made from durable materials to ensure optimal performance and safety.

Vacuum Valve Gate

Our vacuum valve gates are designed to control the flow of materials in vacuum chambers. They provide precise control and reliability for various industrial processes.

Other Custom Parts

We offer a wide range of high-quality hardware parts for vacuum chambers in the semiconductor industry. Our product line includes custom components, motors, automation equipment, and other essential parts necessary for maintaining and optimizing processes within vacuum systems. With our expertise and commitment to excellence, we deliver reliable solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.

Our Services

  • Custom design and fabrication of vacuum chamber components
  • Refurbishment and replenishment of high-quality components for PVD system.

  • Supply of high-quality vacuum-related parts and equipment
